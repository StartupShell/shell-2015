Demo Day

The Event

At Demo Day, Startup Shell members will present their ventures, demo their products, and discuss their progress to fellow members, investors, friends and family. Awesome people are building awesome products and we want to share what we have been working on with the public.

Logistics

Date: April 30th, 2015

Time: 4:00 - 8:00 pm

Address: Startup Shell, 387 Technology Dr, Rm #1100, College Park, MD 20740

Parking is free in non-metered spaces in Lot CC1 (outside of our building) after 4pm. If planning on arriving earlier, there is a visitor parking lot on Paint Branch Drive, just past the Kim Engineering Building. After parking, walk to the North side of the lot, make a left on Technology Drive, and our building is the first on the left.
